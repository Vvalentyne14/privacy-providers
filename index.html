Privacy Policy

Privacy and data Protection

Effective Date: October 16, 2024

1. Introduction

This policy outlines how Mavride] collects and uses information from Providers (organizations coordinating transportation).

2. Information We Collect

From Providers, we collect:

  • Personal Information: Name, business details (company name, address), contact information, trip coordination history, drivers details and payment details.
  • Non-Personal Information: App usage data and analytics.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To manage and approve transportation requests.
  • To communicate with Drivers and Brokers about trip coordination and payments.
  • To process payments related to the services provided.

4. Sharing Your Information

We may share your data with:

  • Drivers: To manage trip assignments and services.
  • Brokers: To approve trip-related payments.
  • Members: To coordinate transportation services.

5. Data Retention

Your data will be retained for as long as you provide services through the app and for legal or compliance requirements.

6. Your Rights

  • You can access or update your data.
  • Request data deletion.
